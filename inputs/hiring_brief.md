# Hiring Brief — Engineering Manager, Platform Engineering

## Role
Engineering Manager — Platform / SRE / DevOps
Remote (Europe), full-time
Reports to: Director of Engineering

## Must-haves
- 3+ years in Engineering Management or equivalent leadership role
- 10+ years total experience in software engineering or infrastructure
- AWS hands-on experience (compute, storage, networking, IAM)
- Experience leading SRE, DevOps, or Platform Engineering teams
- Track record of building internal developer platforms or developer experience
- Hands-on familiarity with CI/CD, IaC (Terraform), containers (Docker)
- Strong English (written and spoken)
- Comfortable working remote-first and async

## Nice-to-haves
- Kubernetes in production (not just basics)
- Service mesh or GitOps tooling (Istio, Linkerd, ArgoCD)
- Experience operating data platforms (Kafka, Spark, streaming)
- Public technical writing or conference speaking
- AI-assisted engineering practices in team workflows
- Hiring and team-building experience

## Out of scope
- Pure individual contributor roles
- Backend product development (this is platform-focused)
- Frontend
